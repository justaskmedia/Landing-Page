What is iCBT?

iCBT is Internet-based Cognitive Behaviour Therapy (CBT). CBT is the most effective and commonly used therapy for a wide range of mental health concerns. The ‘C’ is for cognitive and refers to what and how we think. The B is for behaviour or how we act. Put together Cognitive-Behavioural is about the relationship between our thoughts, our emotions, and our behaviours.

Those thoughts, emotions, and behaviours connect to our moods, our physical experiences, and events in our lives. Being aware of how negative thoughts about an event or experience might be affecting how we act, or perceive a situation, can change our emotional, behavioural and physical responses to it.

AbilitiTherapy by Morneau Shepell is a leading online iCBT program. We combine a personalized therapist-guided program with online tools and exercises to bring CBT to life whenever and wherever you are.

AbilitiTherapy may be covered by your Health Insurance Benefits

AbilitiTherapy is an iCBT program delivered by a qualified therapist. It is, therefore, eligible to be reimbursed by most paramedical plans in Canada. In addition, it is eligible for reimbursements under most Health Spending Accounts.

With some benefits plans offering as little as $500 a year for counselling, it can be challenging to find options that meet your budget. At $400/program AbilitiTherapy is a low-cost alternative to traditional in-person therapy, which can exceed $2000 for a course of Cognitive Behavioural Therapy.

AbilitiTherapy has been validated to be eligible for reimbursement by Great-West Life, SunLife, and The Co-Operators. For all other insurers, please check with your benefits provider.

How it works

All programs will start with a clinically validated screening assesses for depression, anxiety, substance abuse, mania, sleep, and other mental heath conditions.

The therapist will review the screening results to create a personalized program.

Each program contains a series of modules that follow an established CBT framework: assessing your current state, education and lesson, skill building, reinforcement and reassessment The therapist will guide you throughout by reviewing and providing feedback on all exercises and modules. When you are ready the therapist will make the next module available in your program.
