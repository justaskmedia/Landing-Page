iCBT by Morneau Shepell User Guide

Accessing iCBT by Morneau Shepell

To access the iCBT program at any time, log into http://www.MyICBT.com and click “Log In”.

You should use the email and password you created in your sign up.

If you have forgotten password, simply click “forgot password” to reset.

Navigating the program

Once inside your file, you will see a few notes on making the most of your therapy program. Click the button that says ‘next step’ then select the program that has been unlocked for you to get started.

This will bring you to your Progress Tracker.

When you complete steps in the module they will get a checkmark next to them.

The title will become a hyperlink, any time you need you can go back and reread or redo a step.

Your therapist will open one module at a time.

To start on the module, click the “Start” on Module 1

Inside the module

Each module contains activities, videos and assignments to help you learn, develop and practice new skills. Activity workbooks are seen by your therapist who will review and provide direction and support.

To get back to the progress tracker at any time, click “Return Home” at the bottom of each step

At any time you can contact your therapist. Do this by clicking “send a message” at the bottom of any page. Your therapist will be reviewing your progress and will be sending you messages throughout.

When you have a message you will get a bright red alert.

To read and respond, simply click “Send Message”

Your therapist keeps regular business hours so will respond to your message when they are in office- but if you ever need immediate support, our 24/7 clinical call centre number is noted at the bottom of each page

You will have access to iCBT by Morneau Shepell for a full year, and we encourage you to continue to repeat exercises as the need arises.

You have taken an important step and we hope you find iCBT by Morneau Shepell as a helpful tool in making positive behaviour changes to feel better and reach your goals.
